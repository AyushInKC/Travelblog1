About Me

Hi, I'm Ayush Manoj Singh, a passionate traveler and a sophomore in Data Science at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College. Born and raised in the culturally rich city of Varanasi, I have always been fascinated by the beauty of diverse landscapes, the warmth of different cultures, and the stories that travel brings. This blog is my personal space where I share my adventures, experiences, and the lessons I've learned along the way.


As someone deeply interested in backend development, I've infused my technical skills into creating this travel journal website, blending my love for coding with my passion for exploration. When I'm not traveling, you can find me diving into a good book or enjoying music that inspires me to discover new places.


Join me as I embark on exciting journeys, discover hidden gems, and document my adventures in this travel journal!
